My Favorite Destinations

Below are some of the destinations I've cherished, along with must-do activities and sights to explore during your visit.

  1. London, UK
    • Tower of London and Big Ben.
    • Walk around Hyde Park.
    • Westminster Abbey
  2. Tokyo, Japan
    • Walk around the gardens of Shinjuku Gyoen.
    • Enrich yourself with Japanese food in Harajuku.
    • Walk around the Shibuya neighborhoods
  3. Rome, Italy
    • Visit Saint Peters Basilica
    • Pantheon
    • Tour the ruins of the Colosseum
  4. Cairo, Egypt
    • Visit Pyramids of Giza & the Sphinx.
    • Sail along the Nile River.
    • Check out the Valley of Kings
